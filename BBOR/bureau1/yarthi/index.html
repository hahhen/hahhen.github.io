WHAT IS YARTHI

Yarthi is a fan-made cover album by Hahhen. The songs come mainly from Yandhi, a scrapped project by Kanye West, which would later become Jesus is King. A member of the Yedits community, u/msszero159, made a mashup album combining songs from both albums, which directly impacted in A's (or Hahhen's) life.

The title Yarthi comes from a mix of the original title, Yandhi, and A's name: Arthur. Hahhen makes his rendition of msszero's fan-made album in this self-pouring project.
